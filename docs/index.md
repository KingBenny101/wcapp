# Home

Welcome to the Web Gallery of Incalulas's Wallpaper Collection!

## How to Use

1. **Browse Categories**: Use the navigation tabs above to explore different wallpaper categories
2. **View Wallpapers**: Click on any thumbnail to view the full-size image
3. **Download**: Right-click on the full-size image and select "Save image as..." to download

## Categories

- **Space** - Cosmic scenes, nebulae, planets, and astronomical wonders
- **Anime** - Stylized illustrations and anime-inspired artwork
- **Landscape** - Natural scenery, mountains, forests, and scenic views
- **Mythological** - Fantasy creatures, mythical beings, and legendary scenes
- **Realistic** - Photographic and photorealistic images
- **Uncategorized** - Mixed collection of various themes
